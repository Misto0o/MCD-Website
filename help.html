üßÆ The Math Behind It

Effective Mob Damage Formula:
Mob Damage = (Trial Multiplier √ó Mob Health Multiplier) √∑ Armor Ratio
Armor Ratio = (Your Armor Health + 100) √∑ (1,917,147)
Effective Mob Health Formula:
Mob Health = (Trial Multiplier √ó Mob Health Multiplier) √∑ Weapon Ratio
Weapon Ratio = (Your Weapon Damage + 100) √∑ (Base Weapon Damage + 100)
üìù Notes:
‚Ä¢ 1,917,147 is the base health for ALL armor at power level 251
‚Ä¢ Base Weapon Damage varies by weapon type (that's why you select it in the dropdown)
‚Ä¢ The +100 is added to both values for proper scaling
‚Ä¢ Mob Health Multiplier = 1 + (Mob Health % √∑ 100)
